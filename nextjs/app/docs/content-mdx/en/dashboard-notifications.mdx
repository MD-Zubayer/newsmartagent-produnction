# 🔔 Notifications

The **Notifications** section in New Smart Agent (NSA) serves as a centralized hub for all critical updates, alerts, and system messages related to your account. Stay informed about every important event across your business ecosystem in real-time.

---

### 📋 1. Types of Notifications

You will receive various categories of alerts formatted for clarity:

*   **💳 Payment Updates**: Get instant confirmation when your payments are verified or alerts if a transaction requires attention.
*   **🎁 Offer & Plan Alerts**: Receive reminders when your current plan is nearing expiration or when exclusive new offers become available.
*   **🤖 AI Agent Activity**: Notifications triggered by significant agent milestones, such as successful lead captures or critical customer interactions.
*   **⚙️ System Announcements**: Important news regarding platform maintenance, feature releases, and service optimization.

---

### 🛠️ 2. Managing Your Notifications

Keep your workspace organized with easy-to-use notification tools:

*   **🔵 Unread Highlighting**: New messages are clearly highlighted so you never miss a beat.
*   **✅ Mark as Read**: Easily clear notifications individually or in bulk once you've reviewed them.
*   **🗑️ Delete & Cleanup**: Remove old or irrelevant notifications to keep your list focused on current priorities.

---

### ⚡ 3. Real-Time Alerts

NSA delivers notifications instantly. Whenever a new event occurs, a badge or red dot will appear on the notification bell in your dashboard, ensuring you stay reactive to your business needs.

---

> [!TIP]
> We recommend checking your notifications at the start of every business day. This ensures you catch any urgent billing or data sync alerts before they impact your operations.
