# Email Verification Process

After submitting your registration form, your account is in a 'Pending' state. To activate it, you must verify your email address. This prevents unauthorized access and ensures we can reach you for important updates.

### 📧 How to Verify

1.  **Check Inbox**: Look for an email with the subject **'NewsSmartAgent Support - Verify Your Email'**.
2.  **Spam Filter**: If you don't see it, check your **Spam**, **Junk**, or **Promotions** folders.
3.  **Click Link**: Open the email and click the large blue **'Verify Email'** button.
4.  **Success Page**: You will be redirected back to the portal with a success message.

### ⏳ Troubleshooting

*   **Link Expired**: Verification links are valid for **60 minutes**. If expired, request a new link from the login page.
*   **Resend Email**: If you didn't receive the email, click 'Resend Verification' on the gateway.

> [!NOTE]
> Ensure the email from `support@newsmartagent.com` is marked as 'Not Spam' to receive future alerts.
