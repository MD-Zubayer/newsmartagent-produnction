# 📧 Email Verification Process

After submitting your registration form, your account is in a **'Pending'** state. To activate it, you must verify your email address.

---

### 📩 How to Verify

1.  **Check Inbox**: Look for an email with the subject **'NewsSmartAgent - Verify Your Email'** or similar.
2.  **Spam Filter**: If you don't see it, check your **Spam**, **Junk**, or **Promotions** folders.
3.  **Click Link**: Open the email and click the large blue **'Verify Email'** button.
4.  **Success**: You will be redirected back to the portal with a success message.

---

### ⚠️ Crucial Verification Rules

#### ⏳ Time Limit
You must click the verification link within **1 hour** of receiving the email. If the link is not clicked within this timeframe, your registration will be automatically canceled, and you will need to start the sign-up process again.

#### 📱 Cross-Device Verification
You can verify your account from any device. For example, if you receive the email on your phone but want to use the dashboard on your computer, simply click the link from your phone, and you will be able to log in on your computer successfully.

> [!TIP]
> Ensure the email from <SupportEmail /> is marked as **'Not Spam'** to receive future alerts.
