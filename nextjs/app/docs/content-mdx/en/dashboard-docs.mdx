# 📄 AI Docs Sheet: Knowledge Management

The **Docs Sheet** in New Smart Agent (NSA) is the second pillar of AI knowledge management. While Spreadsheets handle tabular data, Docs Sheets are designed for large paragraphs, blog posts, company policies, and articles—otherwise known as **"Unstructured Data."**

---

### 📝 1. Data Format & Usage
This section is ideal for descriptive and detailed information:

*   **📖 Descriptive Data**: Detailed logs of company history, service descriptions, or long articles on specific topics.
*   **✍️ Rich Text Editor**: Use tools like bold, italics, underlining, and custom alignment to organize your content professionally.

---

### 📥 2. Data Mobility (Import & Export)
Building your knowledge base is effortless:

*   **📤 Easy Upload**: Import data directly from **Microsoft Word (.docx)**, **PDF**, or **Text** files.
*   **💾 Offline Backup**: Download your organized documents anytime as an offline backup for future use.

---

### 🤖 3. AI Knowledge Scope
Control how your data is accessed by different agents:

*   **🌐 Global Docs**: Information saved here is accessible to all AI agents in your account. Perfect for general company information.
*   **👤 Agent-Specific Docs**: Restricted to a single designated agent. Other agents will not have access to this data, ensuring privacy for specialized tasks.

---

### ⚙️ 4. Workflow

1.  **New Document**: Create a new doc file from the side menu.
2.  **Data Entry**: Type directly into the editor or upload your existing files.
3.  **Sync & Save**: Click the **Save** button to permanently add the information to the AI's memory. Once saved, the AI will immediately start using this document as a reference when answering customer queries.
