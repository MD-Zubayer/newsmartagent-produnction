# 📊 Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard Overview provides a comprehensive real-time view of your AI automation activities, token usage, and performance metrics.

---

### 💳 1. Quick Stats Cards
Monitor your account status at a glance:

*   **💰 Total Tokens**: The total number of tokens currently available in your account.
*   **📅 Active Validity**: The start and end dates of your current subscription plan.
*   **⏳ Remaining Days**: Countdown of days left before your current offer expires.
*   **📉 Total Consumed**: The cumulative number of tokens used to date.
*   **🚀 Total Requests**: Total count of AI requests processed by the system.

---

### 🧠 2. AI Performance Analytics
Deep dive into your automation efficiency:

*   **📝 Chat Summary**: A concise overview of your most important conversations.
*   **📊 Summary Token Cost**: Tokens spent specifically on data extraction or summarizing tasks.
*   **⚡ Avg. Latency**: The average time (in milliseconds) the AI takes to respond to each message.
*   **❌ Failed Messages**: A count of requests that could not be processed successfully.

---

### 📈 3. Visual Data & Charts
Analyze your usage trends through intuitive graphics:

*   **📅 Daily Token Usage**: Line or Bar charts showing daily input and output token consumption.
*   **🤖 AI Model Usage**: Statistics on different AI models used and the number of requests sent to each.
*   **🌐 Platform-wise Report**: Detailed tables and charts showing token usage across Facebook, Messenger, and WhatsApp.

---

### 📝 4. Active Offer Details
Manage your current subscriptions:

*   **🏷️ Plan Name**: e.g., Standard Plan or Business Pro.
*   **📊 Token Status**: Real-time updates on total vs. remaining tokens for the active plan.
*   **📅 Duration**: The total lifespan of the offer and its exact expiration date.

---

### 📜 5. Recent Activity Log
A detailed list (Table Format) of the last 20 messages, including:

*   **🔗 Platform**: Source of the interaction (e.g., Facebook or Messenger).
*   **📊 Input/Output Tokens**: Breakdown of tokens used for incoming and outgoing messages.
*   **⏰ Timestamp**: The exact date and time of the interaction.
*   **✅ Status**: Indicates whether the message was **Successful** or **Failed**.
