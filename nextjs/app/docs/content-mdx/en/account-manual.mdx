# Creating an Account (Manual)

If you wish to register yourself without the help of an agent, you can fill out the registration form manually. This process is straightforward and takes less than 2 minutes.

### ✍️ Form Details

1.  **Full Name**: Enter your name or your business name.
2.  **Phone Number**: Provide an active mobile number starting with `+880`.
3.  **Email Address**: Use an active email address as this will be used for verification.
4.  **Location**: Select your **Division**, **District**, and **Upazila** from the provided lists.
5.  **Created By**: Select **'Self'** from the dropdown menu.

### 🛡️ Set Password

Choose a strong password that includes uppercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Confirm the password to ensure there are no typos.

> [!NOTE]
> Once you submit the form, you will receive a verification email to activate your account.
