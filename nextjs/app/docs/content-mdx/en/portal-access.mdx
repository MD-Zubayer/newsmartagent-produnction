# Portal Access Guide

To start using the NewsSmartAgent platform, you need to access our official web portal. Follow these steps to ensure a secure connection.

### 🌐 Steps to Access

1.  **Open Browser**: Use a modern browser like Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, or Safari for the best experience.
2.  **Enter URL**: Type [**https://newsmartagent.com**](https://newsmartagent.com) in the address bar.
3.  **Verify Security**: Look for the padlock icon next to the URL to ensure your connection is encrypted.

### 📱 Device Specifics

*   **Desktop**: The 'Get Started' button is located at the top-right corner of the homepage.
*   **Mobile**: Tap the hamburger menu (three horizontal lines) and select 'Sign Up' or 'Login'.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Always ensure you are on the official domain to protect your business credentials.
