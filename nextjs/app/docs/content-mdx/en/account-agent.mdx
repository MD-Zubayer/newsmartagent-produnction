# Registration via Agent

If you are opening an account through an authorized NewsSmartAgent representative, the process differs slightly. This ensures proper tracking and rewards for our partners.

### 🤝 Steps for Agent Registration

1.  **Provide Details**: Give your business details (Name, Email, Location) to the agent.
2.  **Agent ID**: The agent will enter their **Unique Agent ID** in the registration form.
3.  **Created By**: The **'Agent'** option must be selected in the "Created By" field.
4.  **OTP Verification**: You will receive an OTP (One-Time Password) on your mobile or email. Provide this to the agent to confirm the registration.

### 🎁 Benefits of Agent Registration

*   **Dedicated Support**: Get a direct point of contact for integration.
*   **Special Offers**: Accounts opened via agents are often eligible for exclusive discounts or extended trials.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Never share your account password with anyone, including agents. They only need the OTP to finalize registration.
