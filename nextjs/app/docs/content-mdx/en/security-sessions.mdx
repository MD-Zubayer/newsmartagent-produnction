# Session Timeouts & Security

For your protection, NewsSmartAgent maintains strict session management to prevent unauthorized access if you forget to log out.

### ⏳ How It Works

*   **Inactivity**: If you are inactive for **2 hours**, your session will automatically expire, and you will be asked to log in again.
*   **Hard Limit**: Even if you are active, sessions are periodically refreshed every **24 hours** to ensure security tokens are up to date.

### 🛡️ Manual Logout

Always click the **'Logout'** button at the bottom of the sidebar if you are using a shared computer or a public Wi-Fi network.

> [!NOTE]
> Session timeouts are a security feature, not a bug. They protect your business leads if your device falls into the wrong hands.
