# AI Agent Selection Guide

NewsSmartAgent allows you to choose from multiple world-class AI models to power your business automation. Each provider has unique strengths tailored to different needs.

### 🤖 Available Providers

1.  **Google Gemini**: Known for its blazing fast speed and integration with Google's ecosystem. Ideal for high-volume customer support.
2.  **OpenAI GPT-4o Mini**: Offers superior reasoning and natural conversation flow. Perfect for complex sales inquiries.
3.  **xAI Grok**: Known for its 'witty' personality and real-time knowledge integration. Great for engaging brand voices.

### ⚙️ How to Switch

*   Navigate to **Dashboard > AI Agent Settings**.
*   Click the **'Provider'** dropdown.
*   Select your preferred model and click **'Save Configuration'**.

> [!TIP]
> You can switch providers at any time without losing your system prompts or custom keywords.
