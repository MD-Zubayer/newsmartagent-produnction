# 🔄 Dashboard Switching & Display Modes

Our platform is designed to provide a flexible and intelligent user experience through **multi-role access** and specialized display optimizations.

---

### 🔑 Dashboard Access & Switching Logic

#### 🚪 Default & Session-Based Entry
Upon your first login, the dashboard corresponding to your registered Account Type (User/Agent) will be displayed. The system then utilizes **Session-Based Memory** to remember your last active dashboard. When you return, you will be automatically redirected to the specific dashboard you were using before logging out.

#### 👥 Multi-Role Access
On the NewsSmartAgent platform, both **User** and **Agent** dashboards are accessible to everyone. You have the freedom to switch between roles at any time based on your needs.

#### 🛠️ How to Switch
You can instantly toggle between dashboards by clicking the **"Agent"** or **"User"** button located in the **Top Bar** of your screen.

---

### 📱 Display Optimization (Mobile vs. Desktop View)

To ensure a seamless experience across all devices, we offer specialized display modes for mobile users:

*   **🖥️ Desktop Mode**: While on a mobile device, clicking the **Desktop** icon or button in the top bar will render the full desktop version of the dashboard.
*   **📱 Mobile Mode**: Similarly, clicking the **Mobile** icon or button will revert the interface to a mobile-optimized view.

> [!TIP]
> **Adaptive Design**: Each mode features a distinct design layout, meticulously crafted to ensure comfort and efficiency, whether you are working on a large monitor or a compact smartphone.
