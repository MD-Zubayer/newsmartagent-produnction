# 🔗 Connect Facebook Page: Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to integrate your Facebook Page with the NSA dashboard and start your AI-driven business automation.

---

### 🛠️ Integration Process

Follow these 6 steps to successfully link your page:

1.  **📍 Step 1: Access the Connect Menu**
    Log in to your NSA Dashboard. From the left-hand sidebar, navigate to **Dashboard > Connect**.

2.  **📱 Step 2: Select Facebook**
    On the Connect page, you will see various social media platforms. Click on the card labeled **Facebook**.

3.  **⚡ Step 3: Initiate Connection**
    Click the **"Connect to Facebook"** button. This will securely redirect you to the official Facebook authentication window.

4.  **👤 Step 4: Account Verification**
    Once the Facebook interface opens, ensure you are logged into the correct profile. Click the **"Continue as [Your Name]"** button to proceed.

5.  **✅ Step 5: Select Your Page**
    A list of pages associated with your account will appear. Check the box next to the specific page you want to automate with AI and click **"Continue"**.

6.  **💾 Step 6: Confirm & Save**
    Review all the requested permissions and click the **"Save"** button. Once the process is successful, a confirmation pop-up will appear. Click **"Got it"** to finalize the integration.

---

> [!NOTE]
> **Permission Note**: You must grant all requested permissions (Manage Pages, Show List of Pages, etc.) for the AI to function correctly.

> [!TIP]
> **Multi-Page Support**: You can select and connect multiple pages at once during the selection process to manage all your business pages from one dashboard.
