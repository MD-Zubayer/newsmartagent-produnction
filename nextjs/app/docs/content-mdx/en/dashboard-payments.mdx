# 💳 Payment History

The **Payment History** section is designed to help you track all payments and transactions associated with your account. From here, you can verify the status and details of every offer you have purchased.

---

### 📋 1. Understanding the Payment List

The payment history table provides a detailed log of every transaction:

*   **📅 Date & Time**: The exact timestamp when the payment was initiated.
*   **💰 Amount**: The total funds processed in that specific transaction.
*   **💳 Payment Method**: The gateway used for the payment (e.g., Bkash, Nagad, Bank, or Profile Balance).
*   **🔢 Transaction ID**: The unique identifier for your payment.
*   **🚦 Status**: Monitor the current state of your payment:
    *   **✅ Success**: Payment has been verified, and the offer is active.
    *   **⏳ Pending**: Your payment is currently awaiting manual verification by our team.
    *   **❌ Failed/Rejected**: The payment was not successful or was declined.

---

### 📥 2. Downloading Invoices

For your accounting convenience, an invoice is generated for every successful payment:

*   **📄 Payout Link**: A link is provided next to each transaction. Click it to view and download your official payment slip or invoice.

---

### 🛡️ 3. Payment Security & Support

*   **🔒 Secure Transactions**: Our payment verification process is highly secure. For manual transfers, our team cross-references Transaction IDs with extreme care.
*   **🆘 Support**: If your payment is successful but the offer does not activate promptly, please contact our support team with your Transaction ID for immediate assistance.

---

> [!TIP]
> When using manual payment methods, ensure you input the **Transaction ID** accurately. Providing the correct ID ensures the fastest possible activation of your chosen plan.
