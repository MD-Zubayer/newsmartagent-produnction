# Facebook Page Integration

Connecting your Facebook Page is the first step to automating comment replies and lead extraction.

### 🌐 Basic Connection

1.  **Grant Permissions**: Go to **Platforms** and click **Connect Facebook**. You will be redirected to the Facebook login portal.
2.  **Select Pages**: Choose the specific Pages you want to authorize NewsSmartAgent for.
3.  **Enable Features**: Ensure you grant permission for 'Manage Commands', 'Messaging', and 'Page Public Content'.
4.  **Page ID**: Once connected, your Page ID will automatically appear in your dashboard.

### 📝 Access Tokens

Access tokens are generated automatically during connection and are stored securely with encryption. If you disconnect and reconnect, a new token will be issued.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> To maintain the connection, do not change your personal Facebook password frequently, as it might invalidate the access token.
