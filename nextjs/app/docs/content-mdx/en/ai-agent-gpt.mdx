# OpenAI GPT-4o Mini Setup

OpenAI's GPT-4o Mini is a powerhouse for conversational AI, offering unmatched reasoning and natural language understanding for business support.

### 🛠️ Configuration

1.  **Select Model**: In your AI settings, choose **GPT-4o Mini**.
2.  **Context Management**: This model excels at long conversations, so you can provide detailed **System Prompts**.
3.  **Creative Freedom**: Use a **Temperature** of **0.7** if you want more natural, less repetitive responses.

### 🌟 Why Choose GPT-4o?

*   **High Intelligence**: Best for complex customer queries that require logic.
*   **Natural Flow**: Conversations feel more 'human-like' compared to simpler models.
*   **Reliability**: Backed by OpenAI's robust infrastructure.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> GPT-4o Mini may consume slightly more credits per interaction due to its advanced processing power.
