# P2P Balance Transfer

Transfer credits between NewsSmartAgent accounts instantly. This is useful for distributing balance to agents or partners within your business network.

### 💸 How to Transfer

1.  Go to **Billing > Balance Transfer**.
2.  **Receiver Detail**: Enter the recipient's **Unique NSA ID** (e.g., NSA-12345).
3.  **Amount**: Specify the amount in BDT (Min. 100 BDT).
4.  **Confirm**: Enter your secure 4-digit PIN and click 'Transfer Now'.

### 🛡️ Secure Protocol

*   **Verification**: The recipient's name is displayed before you finalize, ensuring the ID is correct.
*   **Zero Fees**: P2P transfers between NewsSmartAgent users are completely free.

> [!CAUTION]
> Transferred balance is non-refundable. Double-check the recipient ID before confirming the transaction.
