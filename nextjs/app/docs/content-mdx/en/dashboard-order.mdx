# 📦 Order Center

The Order Center in New Smart Agent (NSA) is a powerful e-commerce management hub. It allows you to oversee your entire sales pipeline—from initial customer inquiry to final product delivery.

---

### 🛒 1. Smart Order Form (AI Integrated)
Streamline your sales with our automated ordering system:

*   **🔗 Unique Order Link**: Each user gets a dedicated **Share Order Form** link located at the top of the page.
*   **🤖 AI Automation**: If enabled in your settings, the AI agent will automatically send this link to customers interested in purchasing your products.
*   **📝 Data Collection**: The form captures essential details including **Customer Name, Phone Number, Location (District/Upazila), Product Name, Price, Detailed Address**, and any additional notes.

---

### 📋 2. Order List & Management
Stay organized with a comprehensive view of your business transactions:

*   **🔄 Dynamic Order Status**: Track and update the journey of every order:
    *   **🕒 Pending**: Newly received orders awaiting action.
    *   **🚚 Shipped**: Orders that have been handed over to the courier.
    *   **✅ Delivered**: Successfully completed orders received by the customer.
*   **🖨️ Batch Printing**: Select specific orders or the entire list to generate print-ready documents for your logistics team.
*   **🔍 Search & Filtering**: Quickly locate customers using the search bar or filter orders by **District** for easier delivery planning.

---

### 📊 3. Order Analytics & Insights
Visualize your business growth with real-time data:

*   **📦 Total Volume**: Total number of orders received.
*   **🎯 Success Rate**: The percentage of orders successfully delivered.
*   **⌛ Awaiting Action**: A quick count of orders currently in the **'Pending'** stage.
*   **📈 Order Trajectory**: A visual timeline showing daily order trends.
*   **🍩 Status Split**: A donut chart providing a clear ratio of Delivered, Shipped, and Pending orders.
