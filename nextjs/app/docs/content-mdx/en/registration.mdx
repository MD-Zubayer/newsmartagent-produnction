# 📝 Account Registration Guide

To access the AI automation services and the full features of our dashboard, you must first register an account. Unauthorized access is restricted to ensure security.

---

### 🛠️ Method 1: Manual Registration

1. Click on **"Get Started free"**, **"Sign In > Create an Account"**, or any **"Create Account"** link on the website.
2. For mobile users, tap the **Menu (☰)** icon and select **Sign In > Create an Account**.

#### 📝 Complete the Registration Form:
*   **Full Name:** Enter your name or business name.
*   **Contact Info:** Provide a valid Phone Number and Email address.
*   **Location:** Select your Division, District, and Upazila from the dropdowns.
*   **Account Creator (Created By):** 
    *   Select **'Self'** if you are registering personally.
    *   Select **'Agent'** if being assisted by a representative (requires Agent ID and OTP).
*   **Account Type:** Choose **User** or **Agent** (you can access both dashboards with one account).
*   **Password:** Create a strong password using a combination of uppercase letters, numbers, and symbols.

3. Click **"Register Account"** to finalize the form.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Email Verification:** After registering, a verification link will be sent to your email. Click the link to activate your account and log in.

---

### 🤖 Method 2: AI Bot Registration (Automated)

1. Click the **Bot Icon** visible on the screen.
2. An interactive message window will appear, and our AI bot will guide you step-by-step.
3. Provide the required information as prompted by the bot.
4. Once the bot completes the setup, check your email for the **Verification Link** to activate your subscription.
