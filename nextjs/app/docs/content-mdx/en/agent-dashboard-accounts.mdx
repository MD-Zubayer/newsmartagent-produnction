# 💳 Account: Financial Hub & Transactions

The **Account** section, or **Financial Hub**, is your transparent dashboard for managing earnings, tracking balances, and processing secure cashouts on the NSA platform.

---

### 💰 1. Balance Overview (Balance Types)

Your funds are categorized by their source and current status for maximum clarity:

*   **🎁 Commission Balance**: Total earnings generated from your successful referrals and sales activity.
*   **🏦 Account Balance**: Primary account funds, including direct top-ups or peer-to-peer transfers.

**Balance Status Indicators:**
*   **✅ Available**: Funds ready for immediate withdrawal or platform use.
*   **🎉 Success**: Total amount successfully withdrawn to your payment methods.
*   **⏳ Pending**: Requests currently being verified and processed by the system.
*   **❌ Failed**: Requests canceled due to incorrect details or technical errors.

---

### 💳 2. Managing Payment Methods (Saved Methods)

Simplify your withdrawal process by pre-saving your preferred financial channels:

1.  **➕ Add New**: Click the plus (+) icon to link a new secure payment method.
2.  **🏦 Supported Channels**: bKash, Nagad, Rocket, Bank Transfer, or Debit/Credit Cards.

---

### 🚀 3. How to Request a Cashout

Follow these simple steps to move your funds from NSA to your personal account:

1.  **📍 Initiate**: Click the **'Request Cashout'** button at the top right of the dashboard.
2.  **📂 Select Source**: Choose whether to withdraw from your **'Commission'** or **'Account'** balance.
3.  **🏦 Choose Method**: Select one of your pre-saved payment methods.
4.  **💵 Enter Amount**: Input the amount to withdraw (Minimum limit: **৳500**).
5.  **✅ Confirm**: Submit your request. Funds are typically sent after a brief verification.

---

> [!CAUTION]
> **Warning**: Always double-check your account numbers and details before confirming a withdrawal. NSA is not responsible for funds lost due to incorrect user-provided information.

> [!TIP]
> To ensure the fastest payouts, keep your primary banking or mobile wallet information updated in the 'Saved Methods' section.
