# YouTube Comment Automation

Automating your YouTube comments helps you engage with your community faster and drive more traffic to your business links.

### 🎥 Integration Steps

1.  **Grant Access**: Go to **Platforms > Connect YouTube**. Sign in with your Google account.
2.  **Select Channel**: Choose the channel you want the AI to monitor.
3.  **Permissions**: Allow NewsSmartAgent to 'Manage YouTube comments'.

### 📝 Automation Rules

*   **Positive Sentiment**: Reward positive commenters with a 'Thank You' and a coupon.
*   **FAQs**: Answer questions about pricing or production automatically.
*   **Link Sharing**: Automatically share your website or WhatsApp link for further inquiries.

> [!NOTE]
> YouTube automation works best when you set the AI's personality to 'Helpful Creator' for a more personal touch.
