# Profile & User Settings

Customize your NewsSmartAgent experience and keep your account details up to date.

### 👤 Profile Management

*   **Edit Name**: Update the name displayed across the platform and in reports.
*   **Phone Change**: For security, changing your phone number requires OTP verification.
*   **Avatar**: Upload a business logo or personal photo to represent your account.

### ⚙️ Preference Settings

1.  **Notification Center**: Choose to receive alerts via email or platform notifications for new leads.
2.  **Language**: Set your default dashboard language (EN/BN).
3.  **Timezone**: Adjust to your local timezone for accurate reporting.

> [!TIP]
> Keep your **Business Name** updated as it is used automatically by AI agents when introducing themselves to customers.
