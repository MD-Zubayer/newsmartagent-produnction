# Spreadsheet Synchronization

NewsSmartAgent allows you to sync your smart leads and orders directly with Google Sheets for advanced data analysis and offline access.

### 📊 Setup Sync

1.  Navigate to **Dashboard > CRM > Sync Settings**.
2.  Click **'Connect Google Sheets'**.
3.  Authorize NewsSmartAgent to manage specific folders in your Google Drive.
4.  A new sheet named **'NSA_Leads_YourPageName'** will be created.

### 🔄 Automatic Updates

Any new lead identified by the AI is instantly added as a new row in your spreadsheet. You don't need to manually copy or paste data.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Do not delete the headers (first row) of the generated spreadsheet, as this might break the synchronization process.
