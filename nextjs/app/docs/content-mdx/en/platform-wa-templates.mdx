# WhatsApp Message Templates

To stay compliant with WhatsApp's policies and prevent spam reports, use **Message Templates** for initiating conversations with your leads.

### 📝 What are Templates?

Templates are pre-approved messages that can include variables like {"{{name}}"} or {"{{order_id}}"}.

### 🚀 Creating a Template

1.  Go to **CRM > Messaging Templates**.
2.  Click **'New Template'**.
3.  **Category**: Choose 'Utility' or 'Marketing'.
4.  **Body**: {"Hi {{1}}, thanks for your interest in {{2}}. Your order status is {{3}}."}
5.  **Submit**: Wait for 24-48 hours for Meta approval.

### 🤖 AI Integration

Once a template is approved, your AI Agent can automatically trigger it when a specific event occurs (e.g., a new order is marked as 'Shipped').

> [!TIP]
> Templates with interactive buttons (e.g., 'Visit Website') significantly increase customer engagement rates.
