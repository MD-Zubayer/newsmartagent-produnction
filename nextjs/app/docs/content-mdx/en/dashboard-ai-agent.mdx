# 🤖 AI Agent Management: Control & Customization

The AI Agent page is the brain of your automation ecosystem. Every time you connect a platform (like WhatsApp or Messenger), New Smart Agent (NSA) automatically creates a dedicated **AI Agent**.

---

### 📋 1. Agent Overview & Status

Keep track of all your virtual assistants in one unified dashboard:

*   **🟢 Active Agents**: Assistants currently live and interacting with your customers.
*   **🔴 Deactive Agents**: Assistants that have been temporarily paused or disabled.

---

### ⚙️ 2. Core Configuration (Edit Agent)

Fine-tune how your AI thinks and communicates by adjusting these key parameters:

1.  **🏷️ AI Agent Name**: Give your agent a specific name for easy internal tracking.
2.  **🧠 Select Model (The Brain)**: Choose from high-performance models (e.g., GPT-4, Gemini, Grok) based on your plan. This defines the quality of logic and reasoning.
3.  **🎭 System Prompt**: Define the agent’s personality, role, and tone.
    *   *Example: "You are a friendly sales expert for a luxury fashion brand."*

---

### 🛠️ 3. Advanced Technical Controls

Optimize performance and manage operational costs with precision:

*   **📜 History Limit**: Set how many previous messages the AI should remember to maintain conversational context.
*   **🌡️ Temperature**: Control creativity. Low values result in factual, "to-the-point" answers; high values allow more creative flair.
*   **💎 Max Tokens**: Limit response length to keep interactions concise and save on token usage.
*   **⏭️ Skip History**: Configure specific scenarios where the AI should ignore past context for a fresh start.

---

> [!TIP]
> **Pro Tip**: Use a detailed System Prompt to include your business hours, return policies, or specific product knowledge. This drastically improves the AI's helpfulness.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Auto-Creation**: New agent cards are generated automatically the moment you link a new platform via the Connect page. There's no need for manual setup!
