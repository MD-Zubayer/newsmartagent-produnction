# Refund & Credit Policies

Managing refunds fairly and efficiently is key to maintaining customer trust. NewsSmartAgent provides a streamlined way to handle credit reversals.

### 💳 Refunding a Payment

1.  Navigate to **Billing > Balance History**.
2.  Find the specific transaction and click **'Request Refund'**.
3.  **Approval**: Our finance team will review and reverse the credits to your account within 24 hours.

### ⚠️ Policy Terms

*   **Credits**: Non-withdrawable once used for a subscription.
*   **Balance**: Refundable to original payment method (Bkash/Nagad) only if the balance was added in error and not used.

> [!CAUTION]
> Account balances are strictly non-transferable between different users without prior written approval from NSA Support.
