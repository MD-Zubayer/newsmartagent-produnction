# Courier Status Synchronization

NewsSmartAgent integrates with top courier services in Bangladesh to provide real-time tracking for your customers.

### 🔗 Connecting a Courier

1.  Go to **CRM > Courier Settings**.
2.  Choose your provider (e.g., RedX, Pathao, Steadfast).
3.  Enter your **API Key** or **Sender ID**.
4.  **Sync**: Your orders will now automatically update their status based on the courier's delivery progress.

### 🤖 AI Notifications

Your AI Agent can be configured to automatically send a WhatsApp or Messenger update to the customer as soon as the courier marks the package as **'Out for Delivery'**.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Ensure your API keys are valid. If a sync fails, check the **Integration Logs** for the exact error message.
