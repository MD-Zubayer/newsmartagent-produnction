# 🕒 Usage History

The **Usage History** section in New Smart Agent (NSA) is designed to provide a comprehensive log of your AI agents' activities. From here, you can track exactly when your AI interacted with customers, which agent was active, and how many tokens were consumed.

---

### 📜 1. Detailed Interaction Logs

The Usage History table provides critical data points for every interaction:

*   **📅 Date & Time**: The exact timestamp when the AI interaction occurred.
*   **🤖 AI Agent**: Identifies which of your specific agents was active during the session.
*   **👤 Customer ID**: The unique ID or name of the customer interacting with the AI.
*   **📊 Token Consumption**: The precise number of tokens deducted from your balance for that session.
*   **💬 Message Count**: The total number of messages exchanged during the conversation.

---

### 🔍 2. Advanced Search & Filtering

If you have a high volume of interactions, you can use our built-in tools to find specific data quickly:

*   **🔎 Search Bar**: Instantly find logs by searching for Customer IDs or Agent names.
*   **📅 Date Filters**: Narrow down your history by specific dates or custom time ranges.

---

### 📉 3. Performance Analytics

Beyond just a list, Usage History serves as a powerful tool for business optimization:

*   **💡 Behavior Analysis**: Understand peak hours when customers are most active.
*   **💰 Budgeting**: Keep a close eye on your token usage to manage your monthly AI service costs effectively.

---

> [!TIP]
> Regularly reviewing your Usage History helps you monitor the quality of AI-customer interactions. If you notice any unusual token spikes, we recommend auditing your AI’s knowledge base and settings.
