Settings: Automation & Security
English Version
The Settings panel is the control center of your NSA experience. It allows you to fine-tune your AI’s behavior, automate subscription renewals, and fortify your account security.

1. Automation Settings
Achieve full autonomy for your business with these intelligent toggles:

Order Link Logic (Enable): When activated, the AI identifies purchase intent and automatically sends your unique order form link to the customer, streamlining the sales process.

Auto Renew Offer: A fail-safe mechanism for your service.

How it works: When your account balance hits a low threshold (e.g., 2,000 tokens), the system automatically purchases your pre-selected plan using your profile balance.

Per-Agent Granular Controls:

Auto Reply: Toggle the AI's ability to respond automatically for specific agents.

History Skip: Define specific keywords; if a message contains these, the AI will ignore previous conversation history for that specific interaction.

History Limit: Set the maximum number of previous messages the AI should "remember" to maintain context.

Temperature & Max Tokens: Control the AI's creativity level (Temperature) and limit the length of each response (Max Tokens) to manage costs.

2. Security & Account Protection
Protect your business data with industry-standard security features:

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Enhance your login security. Once enabled, a unique OTP (One-Time Password) is sent to your registered email for every login attempt. Your account remains inaccessible without this code, even if your password is compromised.
