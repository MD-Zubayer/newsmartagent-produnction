# 👤 Choosing Your Account Type

To cater to different needs, our platform offers two distinct account types. You can choose the one that best fits your goals.

---

### 🛍️ 1. User Account
This account is designed for those who wish to utilize our premium AI automation services.

*   **Who is it for?**: Businesses, content creators, and professionals.
*   **Key Features**: Connect and manage automation for Facebook, Messenger, WhatsApp, and more.
*   **Usage**: Access top-tier tools to streamline your digital presence and customer interactions.

---

### 🤝 2. Agent Account
The Agent account is built for individuals who want to earn by promoting our services.

*   **The Role**: Agents act as partners, introducing new users to the platform and helping them set up their automation systems.
*   **Earning Potential**: When a new user signs up using an Agent’s **ID Number** and **OTP Key**, the Agent receives a **25% commission** on every payment made by that user for the first 5 months.
*   **Scalability**: The more offers a user purchases, the more the Agent earns. If you can manage 100+ users, contact us directly at <SupportEmail /> for a Special Commission Structure.

---

### ⚠️ Important Guidelines

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Exclusive Benefits**: Users who register through an Agent will enjoy **Special Offers and Discounts** consistently.

> [!TIP]
> **Dashboard Switching**: Both account types currently have the flexibility to switch between User and Agent dashboards and enjoy benefits from both.

> [!NOTE]
> **Future Updates**: In the future, we will introduce specific, exclusive features for each type. We recommend choosing an ID type that aligns with your primary long-term goal.
