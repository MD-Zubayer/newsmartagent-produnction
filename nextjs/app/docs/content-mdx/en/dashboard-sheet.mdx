# 📊 Google Sheets Sync

With New Smart Agent (NSA), you can seamlessly sync lead or customer data collected by your AI agent directly to **Google Sheets**. This makes your data management much simpler and more efficient.

---

### 🛠️ 1. How to Connect Google Sheets

You can easily integrate Google Sheets from your dashboard:

1.  Click the **"Connect Google Sheet"** button on your dashboard.
2.  Log in with your Google account and grant the necessary permissions.
3.  Once successfully connected, you will see a list of your available sheets.

---

### 🔄 2. Real-Time Data Syncing

Once connected:

*   **⚡ Automatic Updates**: Whenever the AI collects new customer information (Name, Phone, Address, etc.), it will be instantly added to your spreadsheet.
*   **📂 Organized Data**: Separate columns will be created for each data point, eliminating the need for manual entries.
*   **🔗 Easy Sharing**: You can share this sheet with your team so everyone can view the data in real-time.

---

### 🛡️ 3. Privacy & Control

*   **🔒 Secure Access**: We only write data to the specific sheet you've selected. We do not access any other files in your Google Drive.
*   **🛑 Disconnect Anytime**: You can disconnect your Google Sheets from the dashboard whenever you want. This will stop any further data syncing.

---

> [!TIP]
> For successful syncing, ensure that the first row (Row 1) of your sheet contains column headers (e.g., Name, Phone, Address). This helps the AI understand where to place each piece of information.
