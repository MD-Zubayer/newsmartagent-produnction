# Messenger Bot Setup

Automating your Messenger allows your business to reply to customers instantly, even when you are offline.

### 🤖 Capabilities

*   **Instant Replies**: Respond to common greetings or product inquiries.
*   **Media Support**: Send images, brochures, and links automatically.
*   **Human Handoff**: Seamlessly transition from AI to a human agent when needed.

### 🛠️ Configuration Steps

1.  **Enable Messenger**: In your platform settings, toggle the 'Messenger Bot' switch to ON.
2.  **Define Keywords**: Add keywords like "price", "location", or "কত দাম" to trigger specific automated responses.
3.  **Default Reply**: Set a default response for when the AI doesn't understand the user's intent.

> [!TIP]
> Use a welcoming message like: "Hello! Our AI assistant is here to help. How can we serve you today?" to increase engagement.
