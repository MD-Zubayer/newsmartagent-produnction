# Managing Invoices

Transparency is key. You can view, download, and track all your financial transactions within the NewsSmartAgent portal.

### 📄 Viewing History

1.  Go to **Billing > Invoices**.
2.  A list of all transactions (Deposits and Subscriptions) will appear.
3.  Click the **'PDF'** icon next to any entry to download a formal invoice for your records.

### 🔍 Understanding Your Invoice

*   **Transaction ID**: Unique identifier for tracking.
*   **Date & Time**: Exact moment of the transaction.
*   **Status**: Indicates if the payment was 'Successful', 'Pending', or 'Failed'.

> [!NOTE]
> Invoices are generated automatically and can be used for business accounting or tax purposes.
