# P2P Transfer History

Maintaining a clear log of your balance transfers is essential for business auditing and partner tracking.

### 📋 Viewing Transfers

1.  Go to **Billing > Transfer History**.
2.  A detailed list of all **Sent** and **Received** transfers will be displayed.
3.  Each entry includes:
    *   **Opposite Party**: Name and ID of the sender/receiver.
    *   **Amount**: Credit value transferred.
    *   **Reference**: Note added during the transfer.
    *   **Timestamp**: Date and time of the transaction.

### 📄 Exporting Data

You can export your transfer history as a CSV or Excel file for external accounting.

> [!NOTE]
> All transfers are final. If you encounter a mistake in a transaction, contact our support team with the Transaction ID for assistance.
