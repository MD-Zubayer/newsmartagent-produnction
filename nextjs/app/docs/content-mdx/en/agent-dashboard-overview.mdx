# 📈 Agent Dashboard: Referral & Earnings Overview

The Agent Dashboard in New Smart Agent (NSA) is a dedicated control center for partners and affiliates. It provides real-time tracking of your revenue, referral network progress, and subscription metrics.

---

### 💰 1. Earnings & Wallet (Financial Insights)

Your critical financial data is displayed in prominent cards at the top:

*   **💳 Account Balance**: Total available funds in your NSA wallet (includes topped-up funds + commissions).
*   **🎁 Commission Earned**: A cumulative total of all commissions earned from successful referrals to date.

---

### 🔗 2. Referral Network (Growth Insights)

Monitor the expansion of your network:

| Metric | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **👥 Total Referrals** | Users who created accounts using your unique link. |
| **✅ Active Referrals** | Users who are currently and actively utilizing NSA services. |

---

### 📊 3. Subscription Tracking (Sales Data)

Track the purchasing behavior of your referred audience:

*   **📝 Total Subscriptions**: Overall count of successful plan sales via your link.
*   **🌟 Currently Active**: Users with a subscription plan currently live.
*   **⌛ Currently Inactive**: Users whose plans have expired—ideal for targeted re-engagement!

---

> [!TIP]
> **Earning Strategy**: Reach out to your 'Inactive' referrals to help them choose a new plan. This is the fastest way to boost your recurring commissions!

> [!NOTE]
> All commissions are credited instantly to your wallet. You can use these funds to purchase services or request a payout.
