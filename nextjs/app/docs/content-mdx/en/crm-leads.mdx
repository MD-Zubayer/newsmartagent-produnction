# Viewing Smart Leads

NewsSmartAgent automatically extracts potential customer names, phone numbers, and interests from comments and messages. This data is organized in the 'Smart Leads' section.

### 📊 Accessing Leads

1.  Navigate to **Dashboard > CRM > Smart Leads**.
2.  A paginated table will show all active leads extracted by your AI.
3.  Filter leads by platform (Facebook, WA, YT) or by specific keywords.

### 📝 Lead Details

*   **Identified Name**: The name extracted from the user's profile.
*   **Phone Number**: Extracted if mentioned in the conversation.
*   **Intent**: Summarized by AI (e.g., 'Inquiry', 'Purchase', 'Support').

> [!TIP]
> Use the **'Call Now'** button next to a lead's phone number to connect with them directly from your mobile dashboard.
