# WhatsApp Business Automation

NewsSmartAgent connects to your WhatsApp Business account using our cloud-based infrastructure to provide unmatched speed and reliability.

### 📱 Syncing Your Account

1.  **Scanner Access**: Go to **Platforms > Connect WhatsApp**.
2.  **QR Scan**: Open WhatsApp on your mobile, go to **Settings > Linked Devices**, and scan the QR code displayed on the screen.
3.  **Active Mode**: Do not close the WhatsApp app on your phone for at least 30 seconds after scanning to ensure full sync.

### 🌟 Key Features

*   **Auto-Reply**: Automatically answer FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions).
*   **Lead Extraction**: Extract names and phone numbers directly into your Orders Spreadsheet.
*   **Global Reach**: Connect with customers worldwide at standard API rates.

> [!CAUTION]
> Avoid sending too many unsolicited messages at once to prevent your WhatsApp number from being flagged for spam.
