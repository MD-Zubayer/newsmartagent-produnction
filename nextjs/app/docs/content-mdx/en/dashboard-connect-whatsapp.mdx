# 📲 Connect WhatsApp: Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to link your WhatsApp account with New Smart Agent (NSA) and start providing 24/7 AI-driven customer support.

---

### 🚀 Integration Process

Follow these 4 easy steps to get your WhatsApp connected:

1.  **📍 Step 1: Navigate to the Connect Menu**
    Log in to your **NSA Dashboard**. From the left-hand sidebar, click on the **Connect** button.

2.  **⚡ Step 2: Initiate WhatsApp Session**
    Find the **WhatsApp** card on the Connect page and click it. Then, click the **"Connect Session"** button. Wait a few moments for a **QR Code** to generate on your screen.

3.  **🔗 Step 3: Link from Your Smartphone**
    Open the WhatsApp app on the smartphone you wish to automate:
    *   Tap the **Three Dots (⋮)** menu in the top right corner.
    *   Select **Linked Devices**.
    *   Tap the **"Link a Device"** button (verify with your phone's PIN or biometric if prompted).

4.  **📸 Step 4: Scan the QR Code**
    Point your phone's camera at the computer screen to scan the generated **QR Code**. Once the scan is successful, your account will load and connect to your NSA Agent within a few seconds.

---

### 💡 Pro Tips for Success

> [!TIP]
> **Uninterrupted Service**: To ensure the AI responds to customers 24/7, keep your WhatsApp account linked at all times. Once linked, the agent will function even if your phone goes offline.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Multi-Agent Setup**: You can connect different WhatsApp numbers for different agents using this same process. This allows you to scale your customer service operations effortlessly.
