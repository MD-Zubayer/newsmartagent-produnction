# Enabling Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Add an extra layer of security to your account by enabling 2FA. This ensures that even if someone knows your password, they cannot access your account without a code from your mobile.

### 🛡️ How to Enable

1.  Navigate to **Settings > Security Center**.
2.  Click on **'Enable 2FA'**.
3.  **App Setup**: Use an authenticator app like Google Authenticator or Microsoft Authenticator to scan the QR code.
4.  **Confirm Code**: Enter the 6-digit code shown in the app to verify.

### 🔑 Backup Codes

Once enabled, you will receive a set of **Backup Codes**. Save these in a safe place. They are the only way to access your account if you lose your mobile device.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> 2FA is highly recommended for users managing high-volume leads or large account balances.
