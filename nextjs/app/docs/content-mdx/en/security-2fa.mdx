# 🛡️ Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

To ensure the maximum security of your account, we highly recommend enabling **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**. Even if someone discovers your password, they will be unable to access your account without the unique **OTP (One-Time Password)** sent to your email.

---

### ❓ Why is 2FA Essential?

*   **🛡️ Enhanced Security**: Adds an extra layer of protection beyond just a password.
*   **🚫 Unauthorized Access Prevention**: Keeps your AI automation settings and personal data safe from hackers.

---

### ⚙️ How to Enable 2FA

1.  Navigate to your **Dashboard**.
2.  Go to **Settings > Security** option.
3.  Toggle the **"Two-Factor Authentication"** button to **Enable**.

---

### 🔐 How it Works

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Once enabled, every time you attempt to log in, a unique **OTP Code** will be sent to your registered email address. You will only be granted access to the dashboard after correctly entering this code.

> [!TIP]
> If you don't receive the email promptly, please check your **Spam** or **Junk** folder.
