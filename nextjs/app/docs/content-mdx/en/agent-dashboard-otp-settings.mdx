# 🔐 OTP Settings: Verification & Referral Control

The **OTP Settings** page is a critical security feature designed to validate new user registrations. It ensures that every referral is accurately credited to your account, protecting your hard-earned commissions.

---

### 🛡️ 1. Unique Identifiers (ID & OTP Key)

Your referral security is built on two primary identification elements:

*   **🆔 Agent Unique ID**: Your permanent identification number. When entered during registration, the system automatically recognizes the user as your referral.
*   **🔑 OTP Key**: A dynamic security layer. This key ensures no one can use your referral ID to create an account without your explicit permission or invitation.

---

### 💰 2. Registration & Commission Integrity

To guarantee your commission flow, please ensure the following:

*   **📝 Form Integration**: During sign-up, users must enter both your **Agent Unique ID** and the **Current OTP Key** in the designated referral fields.
*   **✅ Guaranteed Tracking**: Only when both pieces of information are correctly entered will the system link the user to your network, securing your earnings.

---

### ⚙️ 3. Operational & Security Controls

Take full control over your referral credentials with these dashboard tools:

1.  **🔄 Dynamic Refresh**: Click the **Refresh** icon next to the OTP Key to generate a new code instantly. This prevents unauthorized reuse of your credentials.
2.  **📋 Quick Copy**: Dedicated **Copy** buttons allow you to instantly share your ID and OTP with potential customers via chat or email.

---

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Refreshing your OTP Key renders the previous one invalid. Always ensure you provide the latest key to new users who are registering.

> [!CAUTION]
> While your Unique ID is public, keep your OTP Key private and only share it with intentional referrals to maintain the integrity of your network.
