# Adding Account Balance

Your account balance is used to pay for subscriptions and AI API interactions. Keeping your balance topped up ensures uninterrupted service.

### 💵 Deposit Methods

We support a wide range of secure payment methods in Bangladesh:
*   **bKash / Nagad / Rocket**: Instant mobile financial service transfers.
*   **Debit/Credit Cards**: Visa, Mastercard, and DBBL.
*   **Bank Transfer**: For large enterprise deposits.

### 🚀 Topping Up

1.  Navigate to **Billing > Add Balance**.
2.  Enter the amount in BDT (Min. 500 BDT).
3.  Click **'Proceed to Pay'** and complete the transaction on the secure gateway.
4.  Your balance will update instantly upon successful payment.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Always keep a minimum balance of 200 BDT to ensure your AI agents don't stop responding during peak hours.
