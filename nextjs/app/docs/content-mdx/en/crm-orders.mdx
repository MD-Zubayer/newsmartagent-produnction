# Order Tracking & Status

Manage your customer journey from the first inquiry to final delivery directly within the NewsSmartAgent CRM.

### 🛒 Managing Orders

1.  Go to **CRM > Order Tracking**.
2.  All sales extracted by the AI are listed with a **'Pending'** status initially.
3.  Update the status as you move through the fulfillment process:
    *   **Pending**: Order received, awaiting confirmation.
    *   **Shipped**: Dispatched for delivery.
    *   **Delivered**: Successfully received by customer.
    *   **Canceled**: Order invalidated.

### 📈 Reports

Access the weekly and monthly sales reports to track your business growth and AI efficiency.

> [!NOTE]
> Updating order status allows the AI to provide accurate shipping updates to customers automatically.
