# Subscription Plans

NewsSmartAgent offers a variety of plans tailored for startups, growing businesses, and large enterprises.

### 📦 Plan Categories

1.  **Starter**: Best for new businesses. Includes 1 Platform and 1 AI Agent.
2.  **Professional**: For growing brands. Includes up to 3 Platforms and advanced AI models.
3.  **Enterprise**: Unlimited access with dedicated API support and high-volume limits.

### 💳 How to Subscribe

*   Go to **Billing > Membership**.
*   Select your preferred plan.
*   Click **'Subscribe'**. Credits will be deducted from your account balance.

> [!TIP]
> Subscribe for **12 months** to get a 20% discount on any professional plan.
