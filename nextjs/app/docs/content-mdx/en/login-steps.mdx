# 🔐 Secure Login Steps

Once your account is successfully verified, follow these steps to access your dashboard:

---

### 🔑 Login Workflow

1. Click on any **"Sign In"** link available on the website.
2. For mobile users, tap the **Menu (☰)** icon and select the **Sign In** option.
3. Enter your registered **Email address**.
4. Input your secure **Password**.
5. Click the **"Login"** button to successfully access your account.

---

### 🛡️ Best Security Practices

*   **Private Browsing**: Avoid saving passwords on public computers.
*   **Unique Credentials**: Do not reuse passwords from other platforms.

> [!TIP]
> Use a password manager to store your credentials securely and avoid forgetting them.
