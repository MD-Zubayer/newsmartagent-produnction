# Secure Login Steps

Once your account is verified, you can log in to your dashboard. We use industry-standard security to keep your business data safe.

### 🔑 Login Workflow

1.  **Access Gateway**: Go to the login page.
2.  **Email**: Enter your registered email address carefully.
3.  **Password**: Input your password. Use the **'Eye Icon'** to toggle visibility if you suspect a typo.
4.  **Login Button**: Click 'Login' to proceed.

### 🛡️ Best Security Practices

*   **Private Browsing**: Avoid saving passwords on public computers.
*   **Unique Creds**: Do not reuse passwords from other platforms.
*   **Session Timeout**: For security, inactive sessions will automatically log out after 24 hours.

> [!TIP]
> Use a password manager to store your credentials securely and avoid forgetting them.
