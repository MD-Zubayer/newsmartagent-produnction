# Password Best Practices

To keep your NewsSmartAgent account and your business data secure, follow these password guidelines.

### 🛡️ Creating a Strong Password

*   **Length**: Use at least 12 characters.
*   **Complexity**: Include a mix of uppercase (`A-Z`), lowercase (`a-z`), numbers (`0-9`), and special characters (`!@#$%^&*`).
*   **Uniqueness**: Do not use the same password you use for Facebook, Google, or other business portals.

### 🚫 What to Avoid

1.  **Personal Info**: Avoid using your name, phone number, or birthdate.
2.  **Common Words**: Don't use "password123" or "admin".
3.  **Easy Patterns**: Patterns like "qwerty" or "12345" are easily hacked.

> [!TIP]
> Use a passphrase—a sequence of 4-5 random words (e.g., "SkyBlueRunningWaterFast")—which is easy to remember but hard for computers to guess.
