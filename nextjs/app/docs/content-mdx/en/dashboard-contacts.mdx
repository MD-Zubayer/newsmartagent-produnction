# 💬 Contacts & Live Chat: Centralized Messaging Hub

The **Contacts** page of New Smart Agent (NSA) serves as the ultimate command center for all your business communications. It brings together customer interactions from Facebook, WhatsApp, and Web Widgets into a single, unified inbox, allowing you to manage AI activities and human support seamlessly.

---

### 📥 1. Omni-Channel Centralized Inbox

Experience a unified messaging workflow:

*   **🎙️ Unified Messaging**: Access all incoming messages from various platforms (FB, WhatsApp, Web) in one synchronized place.
*   **✉️ Direct Reply**: Select any contact to send a manual reply directly. Your message will reach the customer on the specific platform they are currently using.

---

### ⚙️ 2. Real-Time AI Control (Toggle Switch)

Take manual control whenever you need:

*   **🔘 Instant Toggle**: Next to each contact, you will find a distinct blue icon/toggle. Clicking this instantly disables the AI auto-reply for that specific user.
*   **🚥 Status Indicator**: When the icon is **blue**, the AI is active. When clicked, it turns **red/gray**, signifying that you have taken manual control of the conversation.

---

### 🛠️ 3. Advanced Per-User Configuration

Clicking the **Three-Dot (⋮)** icon at the top of the message window opens specialized settings for that specific customer:

*   **🤖 Auto Reply**: Toggle AI automation specifically for this user.
*   **🚫 Block**: Instantly block any disturbing or irrelevant users.
*   **🧠 User-Specific System Prompt**: Customize how the AI behaves specifically for this customer.
*   **📜 Additional Instructions (Strict Rules)**: Set mandatory rules for the AI in bullet points.
    *   *Example: "Never share our personal phone numbers."*

---

### 🔔 4. Smart Human Intervention Alerts

NSA features an intelligent detection system to ensure high-quality support:

*   **🗣️ Voice Alerts**: If a customer requests human assistance or the AI faces an unrecognizable query, the dashboard triggers a voice alert: *"Please, human is required now."*
*   **🏷️ Human Help Tag**: A **"Human Help"** tag appears next to the message, and the AI auto-reply is automatically paused (turns red).

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Manual Mode Transition**: As soon as you send a manual message, the system switches to **Human Mode**. To resume AI automation, you must manually click the blue toggle again.
