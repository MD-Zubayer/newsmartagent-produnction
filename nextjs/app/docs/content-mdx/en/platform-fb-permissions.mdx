# Facebook API Permissions

To ensure NewsSmartAgent can interact with your fans flawlessly, you must grant specific permissions during the Facebook connection process.

### ⚙️ Required Permissions

*   **pages_manage_metadata**: Allows us to see Page settings.
*   **pages_messaging**: Enables sending and receiving messages in Messenger.
*   **pages_read_engagement**: Allows the AI to see comments on your posts.
*   **pages_manage_posts**: Required for the AI to reply to comments.

### 🛡️ Managing Permissions

If the AI stops responding, visit your **Facebook Business Settings > Integrations** and ensure NewsSmartAgent (owned by NSA Foundations) is still authorized.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> When connecting, do **not** uncheck any of the requested permissions, as even a single missing permission can break the entire automation flow.
