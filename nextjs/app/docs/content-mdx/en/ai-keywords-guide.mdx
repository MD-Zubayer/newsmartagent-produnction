# Custom Keyword Triggers

Keyword triggers are words or phrases that activate specific behaviors in your AI Agent, allowing for high-precision automation.

### 🔑 Setting Up Keywords

1.  Go to **AI Agent > Keyword Triggers**.
2.  Click **'Add New Keyword'**.
3.  **Phrase**: Enter the word or phrase (e.g., "price", "location", "ডেলিভারি চার্জ").
4.  **Action**: Define what the AI should prioritize (e.g., 'Share Price List', 'Ask for Phone Number').

### 🤖 How It Works

When a user mentions a registered keyword, the AI prioritizes the associated action over its general system prompt instructions.

### 🌟 Best Practices

*   **Synonyms**: Add multiple versions (e.g., "cost", "how much", "দাম কত").
*   **Localized**: Include both English and Bengali variations if serving local customers.
*   **Specific**: Use specific keywords like "refund" or "track order" for specialized support.

> [!TIP]
> Use keyword triggers for frequently asked questions to ensure the AI always provides the exact standardized answer your business prefers.
