# Understanding Lead Statuses

Track the progress of every potential customer using the **Lead Status** pipeline in your CRM.

### 🚥 Standard Statuses

1.  **New**: Lead just entered the system via AI interaction.
2.  **In Progress**: You or an agent are currently talking to the customer.
3.  **Interested**: Customer showed high intent to buy or book.
4.  **Closed/Won**: Conversion successful (e.g., order placed).
5.  **Lost**: Customer is no longer interested.

### 🔄 Updating Status

*   **Manual**: Click the status badge in the Lead Details drawer.
*   **Automatic**: AI can automatically mark a lead as 'Interested' if it captures a phone number.

> [!NOTE]
> Keeping statuses updated helps your sales team focus on high-priority 'Interested' leads first.
