# Configuring Google Gemini

Google Gemini is a highly efficient model optimized for speed and reliability. Follow these steps to set it up for your automation.

### 🛠️ Setup Steps

1.  **Select Provider**: Choose **Gemini 1.5 Flash** from the settings.
2.  **API Integration**: Ensure your platform balance has enough credits to cover API calls.
3.  **Response Tuning**: Adjust the **Temperature** to **0.5** for a balance between factual accuracy and natural flow.

### 🌟 Advantages

*   **Fast Response**: Ideal for real-time comment replies on Facebook.
*   **Multi-Lingual**: Excellent understanding of both Bengali and English.
*   **Scalability**: Handles thousands of requests simultaneously with ease.

> [!NOTE]
> Gemini is the default recommended model for new users due to its cost-effectiveness and speed.
