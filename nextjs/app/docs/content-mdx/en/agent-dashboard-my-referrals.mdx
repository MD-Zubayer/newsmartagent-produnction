# 👥 My Referrals: Your Network Database

The **My Referrals** page is a centralized database for agents to monitor and manage their network. It provides real-time access to information about users who joined using your unique credentials.

---

### 📋 1. User Details (The Member List)

The main dashboard table displays key information about each referred member:

*   **👤 User Profile**: Instantly view the registered name and email address of your network members.
*   **📅 Joining Date**: A precise timestamp of when each user completed their registration through your referral.

---

### 📈 2. Activity & Subscription Monitoring

Keep track of your network's growth and engagement with real-time updates:

*   **⚡ Account Status**: Check if a user's ID is currently **Active** or **Inactive** to identify your most engaged followers.
*   **💎 Subscription Insights**: Monitor which users are currently subcribed to a paid plan to better understand your potential commission flow.

---

### 🔍 3. Management & Navigation Features

Streamline your network operations with built-in productivity tools:

1.  **🔎 Smart Search**: Use the top search bar to instantly find specific users by name or email address.
2.  **🗂️ Tabular Interface**: Information is organized in a clean, professional table for ultimate scanability and ease of use.

---

> [!TIP]
> Regularly monitor this page to identify inactive users. Re-engaging them can help you unlock more commission rewards.

> [!NOTE]
> Only users who successfully registered using your **Unique Agent ID**, **Referral Link**, or **OTP Key** will appear in this database.
