# Exporting Smart Leads

NewsSmartAgent allows you to export your lead data for external marketing campaigns (e.g., Facebook Lookalike Audiences) or offline records.

### 📥 How to Export

1.  Navigate to **CRM > Smart Leads**.
2.  **Filter**: Use the search bar or date filters to select the specific leads you want.
3.  **Click Export**: Tap the **'Export CSV'** button at the top right.
4.  **Download**: Your file will be generated instantly and downloaded to your device.

### 📊 Field Mapping

The exported file includes:
*   **Name**: From social profile or AI capture.
*   **Phone**: Verified through conversation.
*   **Platform**: FB, WA, or YT.
*   **Interest Level**: Based on AI sentiment analysis.

> [!TIP]
> Periodically export and upload your leads to **Google Sheets** for long-term storage and advanced business analytics.
