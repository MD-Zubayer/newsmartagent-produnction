# Defining AI Personality

The 'Personality' of your AI Agent is controlled by the **System Prompt**. This is the most critical part of setting up a successful business automation, as it defines how the AI will represent your brand.

### ✍️ Writing the System Prompt

The System Prompt is a set of instructions you give to the AI.

1.  **Identity**: Tell the AI who it is. (Example: "You are the head of customer service for 'New Smart Shoes'.")
2.  **Tone**: Specify how it should speak. (Example: "Maintain a professional yet friendly and welcoming tone.")
3.  **Constraints**: Tell it what NOT to do. (Example: "Do not give discounts unless the customer mentions a 'COUPON'.")
4.  **Goal**: What is its primary mission? (Example: "Your goal is to extract the customer's phone number and location.")

### ⚙️ Adjustment Tools

*   **Temperature**: Lower values (0.2 - 0.4) make the AI consistent and factual. Higher values (0.7 - 1.0) make it more diverse and creative.
*   **Response Length**: You can specify if answers should be short (good for Messenger) or detailed (good for Email).

> [!TIP]
> A well-crafted prompt should always include: **"If you don't know the answer, politely ask for the user's phone number and tell them a human agent will call back."**
