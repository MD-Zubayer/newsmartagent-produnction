# 🎁 Active Offers

The **Active Offers** section in New Smart Agent (NSA) is your central hub for tracking your current membership plan, token limits, and subscription validity.

---

### 📋 1. Offer Details

In this section, you will find a clear breakdown of your current account status:

*   **🏷️ Pack Name**: Displays the name of your current plan (e.g., Standard, Business Pro, etc.).
*   **💰 Purchase Price**: The cost at which the current offer was acquired.
*   **📊 Token Status**: Real-time updates on your total available tokens vs. remaining balance.
*   **⏳ Validity Period**: The exact date of purchase and the expiration date/time.

---

### 📈 2. Subscription Tracking

Monitor your usage patterns easily with intuitive dashboard cards:

*   **⚡ Real-Time Updates**: Your token balance is deducted and updated immediately after every AI interaction.
*   **🔔 Expiration Alerts**: Keep track of exactly how many days are left before your subscription needs renewal.

---

### 🚀 3. Activating New Offers

If you run out of tokens or your validity period expires:

1.  Browse and purchase a new membership plan or offer.
2.  Once purchased, the new plan will instantly appear as your **Active Offer**, replacing the previous one.

---

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Generally, only one offer is active per account. Whether remaining tokens from a previous plan carry over to a new one depends entirely on the specific package you select.

> [!TIP]
> We recommend checking your token balance regularly to ensure your AI services remain uninterrupted during critical operations.
