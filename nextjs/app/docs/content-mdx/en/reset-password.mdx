# Resetting Your Password

If you've forgotten your password, don't worry. You can regain access to your NewsSmartAgent account by following our secure recovery protocol.

### 🔄 Recovery Steps

1.  **Forgot Password?**: On the login gateway, click the **'Forgot Password?'** link below the credential fields.
2.  **Enter Email**: Provide your registered email address to receive a reset link.
3.  **Check Inbox**: Open the recovery email from NewsSmartAgent and click the **'Reset Password'** button.
4.  **Create New Password**: Enter a new, strong password and confirm it.
5.  **Re-Login**: Use your new credentials to access your dashboard.

### 🛡️ Security Note

*   **Temporary Link**: The reset link is valid for **30 minutes** for your protection.
*   **One-Time Use**: Each link can only be used once.

> [!WARNING]
> If you did not request a password reset, please contact our security team immediately at `security@newsmartagent.com`.
